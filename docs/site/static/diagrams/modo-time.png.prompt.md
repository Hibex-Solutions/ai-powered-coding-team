# Diagrama "Modo Time" — Prompt de geração

A imagem `modo-time.png` é gerada com **Google Gemini**, mesmo estilo visual da opção **"Ciborgue"** usada no banner do site (ver `../banners/banner_ciborgue.png.prompt.md`).

## Prompt utilizado

```
Sci-fi cyberpunk timeline illustration in 16:9 landscape aspect ratio.
Dark deep navy background (#080d1a), subtle hexagonal grid and circuit
lines faintly visible in corners, blue-to-purple neon glow atmosphere,
floating data particles, cinematic lighting, professional sci-fi concept
art style, high detail.

Header label at the top in thin glowing futuristic sans-serif text:
"MODO TIME".

Composition: FIVE cyborg characters along a horizontal timeline flowing
LEFT → RIGHT, each with distinct facial features, body type, clothing
silhouette and posture — four DIFFERENT professionals (the senior
engineer appears at positions [1] and [5] as the SAME recurring
character). Between each figure and the next, a vertical glowing strand
of AI neural network (blue-to-purple gradient, particles flowing upward)
receives the delegation and emits a floating holographic artifact panel
whose glow matches the role color. Thin neural network lines weave the
entire sequence into a web of collaboration.

Stage breakdown (each figure shows a small floating numbered badge above
it — [1], [2], [3], [4], [5] — and a thin glowing role caption beneath):

 [1] ENGENHEIRO SÊNIOR — aura EMERALD GREEN (#10b981) —
     delegates "objetivo" → AI column → artifact panel "docs/GOAL.md".
 [2] ANALISTA — aura VIOLET (#7c3aed), distinct new character —
     delegates "especificação" → AI column → artifact panel
     "docs/BUSINESS.md".
 [3] ARQUITETO — aura ELECTRIC BLUE (#4f6ef7), distinct new character —
     delegates "projeção" → AI column → two stacked artifact panels
     "docs/ARCHITECTURE.md" and "docs/SOLUTION.md".
 [4] DESIGNER — aura PURPLE (#a855f7), distinct new character —
     delegates "projeção" → AI column → artifact panel
     "docs/GUIDELINE.md".
 [5] ENGENHEIRO SÊNIOR (visually identical to the character at [1]) —
     aura EMERALD GREEN (#10b981) — delegates "construção" → AI column
     → glowing terminal panel labeled "[SOFTWARE]".

The distinct appearances of stages 2, 3, 4 emphasize that different
professionals occupy different roles; the matching senior engineer at
[1] and [5] shows continuity of the same role across the delivery cycle.

All labels, badges and artifact filenames must be crisp, legible,
futuristic sans-serif. No text outside the specified labels.
```
